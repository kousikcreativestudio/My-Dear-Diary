Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

My Dear Diary is a personal diary and memory app created to help users write, protect, and manage their private memories.

Information We Collect

The app may collect or store the following information when you use it:

  • Email address used for login or account access.
  • Diary entries, titles, moods, tags, dates, and memories created by the user.
  • Photos or videos added by the user to diary memories or vault entries.
  • Profile name and profile photo if added by the user.
  • App preferences such as theme, design settings, streaks, achievements, and backup settings.

How We Use Information

Your information is used only for app functionality, including:

  • Creating and signing in to your account.
  • Saving and syncing your diary memories.
  • Showing mood analytics, timeline, gallery, streaks, and achievements.
  • Providing secure vault and backup features.
  • Improving the user experience inside the app.

Data Storage and Security

My Dear Diary uses Firebase services for authentication and cloud database storage. Data is transmitted using secure HTTPS connections. The app also includes security features such as vault encryption, vault password hashing, auto lock, wrong password lockout, and password-protected backup.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your diary content with advertisers or third-party marketing companies.

User Control

Users can edit or delete diary entries inside the app. Users can also stop using the app at any time. If you need account or data deletion support, contact the developer.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general personal diary use. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Firebase services provided by Google for login and database functionality.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page.
